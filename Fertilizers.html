Fertilizer For Adenium

Adenium plants need a lot of nitrogen to flower, so you can apply a high-nitrogen fertilizer directly to the soil before each blooming period. Here are some fertilizer options for adenium plants:

  • • Water-soluble, slow-release fertilizer Use a 13-13-13 fertilizer once a month during the growing season.
  • • Fertilizer diluted to half strength Feed indoor adeniums every week during spring and summer using a water-soluble fertilizer diluted to half strength.
  • • Half dose of a 20-20-20 plant food Fertilize your desert rose during its growing period (spring and summer) with a half dose of a 20-20-20 plant food. Stop fertilizing during the winter when it goes dormant. You can also try these fertilizer products for adenium plants:
