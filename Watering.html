Watering Adenium

Water generously once they are actively growing. Through its peak growing season, an adenium growing in full sun and in a pot 12-inches in diameter or more should receive deep soaks at least twice a week. Small specimens in pots smaller than 10 to 12-inches, require water three to four times a week.
